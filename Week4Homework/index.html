How to play OSU!

OSU is a free-to-play rhythmic game!

First, create an account with OSU. You then download a song from the OSU! site then you can begin playing the application.

  • The goal is time your "clicks" to the rhythm of the song you are playing along with the visual guide from the circles
  • There are 3 levels of score ranging from:
      • 300 points
      100 points
      50 points
